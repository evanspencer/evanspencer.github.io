About

I'm a senior product analyst at BILL, where I focus on the virtual cards and business payments product. My day-to-day sits at the intersection of product metrics, experiment design, and narrative — turning messy payment data into decisions that matter.

My path in fintech started in international payments before moving into the virtual card space. That background shapes how I think about data: payment flows are complex, causality is hard to isolate, and the business questions are almost always messier than the data.

Methodologically I care most about causal inference — getting the answer right, not just a statistically convenient one. I spend a lot of time thinking about experiment design, observational methods, and how to communicate uncertainty honestly to stakeholders who want certainty.

Lately I've been upskilling at the boundary of data and engineering — using AI-augmented tools to build things I couldn't before, and thinking about what the analyst role looks like when the cost of writing code drops toward zero.
