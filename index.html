About

I'm a senior product data analyst at BILL, focused on virtual cards and small business payments. Most of my work sits between the data and the decision: figuring out what the numbers actually say, and then finding a way to make that land with people who need to act on it.

I came up through international payments before moving into the virtual card space, which gave me a useful skepticism about clean data. Payment flows are messy. Causality is hard to isolate. The business question is almost always more complicated than it first appears.

I care a lot about getting the right answer over the convenient one, which means I spend a lot of time on experiment design, causal inference, and how to talk honestly about uncertainty with people who'd rather not hear about it.

I also build things. Dashboards, data stories, small applications. AI tools have changed how fast I can move, but the instinct to ship something tangible has always been there. I'm interested in what the analyst role looks like as that keeps shifting.
